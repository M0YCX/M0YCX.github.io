## Prototype
Ugly construction:

![feedback model](/assets/images/2025-06/D20250627_2SC2498_ugly_prototype_1.jpg)

Results:
* i/p swr 1.2:1 @ 70MHz
* o/p swr 1.8:1 @ 70MHz, 1.5:1 @ 50MHz
* Gain:
  * 14dB @ 50MHz
  * 13.5dB @ 70MHz
